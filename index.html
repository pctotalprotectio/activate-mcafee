Activate McAfee

Use your 25-digit product key to activate your McAfee subscription through the official activation portal. After activation, download the latest version and install protection on your device.

How to Activate McAfee

  1. Open mcafee.com/activate.
  2. Sign in to your McAfee account.
  3. Enter your 25-digit activation code.
  4. Confirm your subscription.
  5. Download McAfee.
  6. Run the installer.
  7. Complete installation.

Download McAfee

After activation you can download the latest installer for Windows or Mac.
