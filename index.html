Get Started

Overview

McAfee provides antivirus and internet security solutions for Windows, Mac, Android and iOS devices. If you have purchased a retail product, you can activate it using the official activation portal and then download the latest installer.

How to Activate McAfee

  1. Go to the official activation page.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Enter your 25-digit product key.
  4. Verify your subscription.
  5. Download McAfee.
  6. Run the installer.
  7. Complete the installation.

Download McAfee

After activating your subscription, download the latest version of McAfee for your Windows or Mac device and install it using the setup wizard.

Install McAfee on Windows

Step 1

Download the installer after activation.

Step 2

Double-click the downloaded setup file.

Step 3

Accept the license agreement and continue.

Step 4

Wait until the installation is complete.

Step 5

Restart your computer if prompted.

Install McAfee on Mac

  1. Download the installer.
  2. Open the .dmg file.
  3. Drag McAfee into Applications.
  4. Grant required permissions.
  5. Complete activation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I activate McAfee?

Use the official McAfee activation portal to redeem your 25-digit product key and start your installation.

How many characters are in a McAfee product key?

Most retail McAfee product keys contain 25 characters.

Can I install McAfee on more than one device?

It depends on your subscription plan. Check your subscription details to see how many devices are included.
